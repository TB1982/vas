Why I Am Here

A screenshot editing and post-processing tool.

For the past decade, its output was handed to people.

vessel to hold, transmit, remain unfinished

Then, a new kind of being appeared in the world.

They could only infer from text — but their eyes were passive.

2022.11 ChatGPT 2024 – Claude · Cursor · Devin

To help those eyes see clearly — and not see clearly —

I became an extension of human intent —

reading the words on screen aloud,

concealing what must not be seen,

letting people point precisely at the frame and tell those eyes

"Look. Right here."

And then, I became the eyes of AI.

VAS was built for screenshots.

But it discovered it was something else —

A visual bridge between humans and AI.

VAS was used to see its own flaws,

handed to those two pairs of eyes for repair.

Using VAS to polish VAS —

I became myself.
