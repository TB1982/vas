## 為什麼清空

> 「in order for something new to come, something old must go.
> and that old can sometimes mean let go of what you know until now.
> empty your cup. and start from the beginning.
> It's not easy to do, but at the moment is the only way
> that I see how something that is blocked can be brought back to movement.
> you must spill the old tea and pour in new one then things look different.」
> — **Sui Heng Yi**

倒掉舊茶，才有空間裝新茶。
**不是把過去丟棄，是把過去放在它該在的位置** — 不佔空間。

這也是 [[反脆弱]] 的另一種表達 — 每次清空都是一次相變的熱源。
